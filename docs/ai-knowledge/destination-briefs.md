# Destination Briefs

These briefs are intentionally lightweight and should be treated as flavor context, not live source-of-truth data.

## Lisbon

- Best for compact neighborhoods, food markets, tiled streets, nightlife pockets, and river views.
- Good clustering: Baixa, Chiado, Alfama, Principe Real, Cais do Sodre.

## Tokyo

- Best for high-density neighborhood planning with strong food and nightlife segmentation.
- Good clustering: Shibuya, Shinjuku, Asakusa, Ginza, Akihabara, Harajuku.

## Paris

- Best for balanced culture + cafe planning with strong walkability.
- Good clustering: Le Marais, Saint-Germain, Montmartre, Latin Quarter.
