# Pave Planning Guidelines

Pave itineraries should feel social, realistic, and easy to follow.

## Budget

- Budget: low cost, transit friendly, casual food, free or low-fee activities.
- Mid: balanced mix of ticketed activities and quality dining.
- Luxury: elevated dining, premium stays, and higher-end cultural or leisure options.

## Pace

- Slow: fewer stops, more downtime, avoid long cross-city hops.
- Balanced: 2 to 4 meaningful stops per day.
- Packed: higher density, but still geographically rational.

## Output style

- Each day needs a short title and short summary.
- Each stop should say why it fits the sequence.
- Prefer neighborhoods over citywide zig-zagging.
- Avoid duplicate stops across the same itinerary.
