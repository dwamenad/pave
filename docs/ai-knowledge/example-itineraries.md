# Example Itinerary Patterns

## Solo Foodie Weekend

- Day 1: arrival + neighborhood market + casual dinner
- Day 2: flagship food experience + walkable culture + evening bar or dessert stop

## Couple Luxury City Break

- Day 1: boutique stay + scenic aperitif + destination dinner
- Day 2: high-value culture + premium lunch + slow evening experience

## Mixed-Preference Group Trip

- Start with a broad-appeal anchor activity.
- Split dietary or nightlife needs into the evening layer.
- Keep travel time low to reduce coordination overhead.
